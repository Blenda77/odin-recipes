Fajita Recipe

Description

Fajitas are a Tex-Mex dish of grilled meat and vegetables served with tortillas. They are often paired with toppings like salsa or guacamole.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Vegetables
  • Seasoning
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Slice your chosen protein (chicken, beef, or shrimp) into strips and marinate with fajita seasoning, lime juice, and a bit of oil for at least 15-30 minutes.
  2. Slice bell peppers and onions into strips.
  3. Heat a bit of oil in a pan over medium-high heat and cook the marinated meat until fully cooked and browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. In the same pan, add the sliced bell peppers and onions and cook until softened and slightly charred, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. arm tortillas, then fill them with the cooked meat and vegetables. Top with your favorite toppings like guacamole, salsa, sour cream, or cheese. Enjoy!
